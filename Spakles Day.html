SPARKLES Day

To cultivate good life habits, all students participated actively in keeping the school environment clean this year. Besides the daily routine of keeping the classroom environment clean, the school introduced the SPARKLES Programme to remind students the importance of keeping the school clean so that they could have a more conducive environment for learning.

SPARKLES Day is a meaningful and engaging way for students to learn about responsibility and gain an understanding of how they can contribute towards their environment.

Everyone of us have a role to keep our environment clean.
