NATIONAL DAY

On 6 August 2021, the school celebrated the nation’s 56th birthday in line with this year’s National Day theme, “Together, Our Singapore Spirit”.

‘Together’ evokes our common Singapore identity and Singaporeans coming together as one united people.
‘Our Singapore Spirit’ calls on Singaporeans to draw new strength from our “can-do” spirit amidst challenging circumstances from COVID-19 and potential disruptions in the new decade. We look to reinvigorate the Singapore Spirit - encouraging Singaporeans to embrace a “dare-to-try” attitude, and reinvent to overturn our natural and geographical constraints.
The teachers carried out a series of workshops that allowed students to think about how each of us embody the Singapore Spirit (Our Spirit of Unity, Our Spirit of Resilience and Our Spirit of Hope) and the traits that we share.

This year, in view of the pandemic, the Observance Ceremony and celebration were carried out in the classrooms. One of the segments was an Interactive Gameshow where students can learn about the 5 Singaporean personalities and some significant historical events that have shaped Singapore.
