Chinese New Year Celebrations

Chinese New Year is the
first day of the New Year
in the Chinese calendar,
which differs from the
Gregorian calendar. It is
also known as the Spring
Festival or the Lunar New Year. Every year is
represented by a zodiac
animal sign. At FHPS, our
students will come
together to celebrate
with their classmates.

This year, due to Safe
Management Measures,
the students celebrated
the event in class via a
virtual celebration.
